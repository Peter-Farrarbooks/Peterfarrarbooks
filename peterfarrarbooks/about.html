About

The Story Behind the Books

Author, photographer, and IT leader with over 30 years of experience in high-stakes environments and creative industries.

Expertise Grounded in Reality

My work as a leadership practitioner and photographer is grounded in a single responsibility: explaining complex systems in a way that is practical and usable. My books bring together real-world leadership experience and structured photography knowledge to help people achieve measurable results.

IT Leadership & The US Senate

I began my career in technology launching a computer sales business in the UK before spending 18 years in IT across the United States. During that time, I progressed to **Senior Network Engineer at the US Senate**.

I had the opportunity to work within demanding, high-stakes environments alongside high-profile figures including **Joe Biden, Hillary Clinton, and Senator Ted Kennedy**. Returning to the UK, I continued in senior roles across global finance and defence, gaining first-hand insight into what drives organizational performance.

That understanding forms the foundation of Leadership: The Culture Fix.

Photography & Business Scaling

Photography developed as my creative counterbalance. I founded **Kenneth Light Studios** in Charlotte, North Carolina, where I trained around 300 photographers on lighting, posing, and editing workflows.

I scaled my own wedding business from $300 bookings to consistently achieving **$3,600 to $4,600 per wedding**. The frustration of finding no single, comprehensive resource for this journey led directly to the 538-page Complete Photography Field Guide.

Life Beyond Work

Beyond the desk and the camera, I'm a fan of 1970s music, live concerts, and cinema. My interests range from rock and pop to modern crossover country, always with an appreciation for the craft behind the art.
