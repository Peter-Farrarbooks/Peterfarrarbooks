Privacy Policy

How this website collects and uses information.

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how peterfarrarbooks.com ("the website") collects, uses, and protects any information that you provide when using this website. Your privacy is important and this policy is intended to be clear and straightforward.

1. Information Collected

This website may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you voluntarily submit via the contact form (name, email address, message)

  • Anonymised analytics data about how visitors use the site (pages visited, time on site, general location by country)

  • Technical information such as browser type and device type, collected automatically

2. How Information Is Used

Information submitted through the contact form is used solely to respond to your enquiry. It is not shared with third parties, used for marketing without your consent, or sold.

Analytics data is used to understand how the website is being used and to improve it. This data is anonymised and does not identify individual visitors.

3. Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies. Analytics cookies, if used, are anonymised and do not track you across other websites. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

4. Third Party Links

This website contains links to third party websites including Amazon. These websites have their own privacy policies and this site accepts no responsibility for their content or practices. Clicking an Amazon link may result in cookies being set by Amazon in accordance with their own privacy policy.

5. Data Storage

Contact form submissions are processed securely. No sensitive personal data (such as payment details) is collected or stored through this website.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to request access to any personal data held about you, request its correction or deletion, and object to its processing. To exercise these rights, please use the contact form.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please use the contact page.
