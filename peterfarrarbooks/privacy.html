Privacy Policy

How personal information is collected, used, and protected when using this website.

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how peterfarrarbooks.com ("the website") collects, uses, and protects any information provided when using this website.

1. Information Collected

This website may collect:

  • Information submitted via the contact form (name, email address, message)
  • Anonymous analytics data (pages visited, general location, usage patterns)
  • Technical data such as browser and device type

2. How Information Is Used

Contact form data is used only to respond to enquiries and is not sold or used for unsolicited marketing.

Analytics data is used to improve website usability and performance and does not identify individual users.

3. Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies. Analytics cookies, if present, are anonymised. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Links

This website links to third-party sites such as Amazon. These sites have their own privacy policies. This website is not responsible for their content or data practices.

5. Data Storage

No sensitive personal data is collected. Contact form submissions are processed securely.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Please use the contact page to make a request.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. The update date at the top reflects the latest revision.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy, please use the contact page.
